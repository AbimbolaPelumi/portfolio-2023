My story

As a self-taught designer, I have a unique perspective and approach to problem solving. My background in electrical engineering taught me the importance of function and logic in design, while my passion for aesthetics drives me to create visually stunning and engaging experiences.
Born and raised in Nigeria, I have always been drawn to the intersection of art and technology. I discovered my love for photography and graphic design whilst pursuing my degree in electrical and electronics engineering. Over time, my curiosity and passion for design continued to grow, and I eventually made the decision to switch to a full-time career path in design.
Over the past 2 years, I have had the opportunity to work with a variety of clients in different industries, ranging from SaaS, finance, logistics, to e-commerce. I have been able to apply my unique perspective to a range of challenges involving user experience and visual design, with a particular focus on building products in startup environments. Currently, I work at PwC as a designer where we explore and leverage existing and emerging technologies like AR/VR to solve problems and build solutions to aid internal processes and for our clients.
In my work, I strive to create digital experiences that are both aesthetically pleasing and highly functional. I believe that the optimal user experience lies at the intersection of functionality and form, and I approach each design challenge with a focus on problem solving and making people's lives better or more effective.
Overall, I pride myself a passionate and dedicated designer and I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to learn and grow. I am excited to continue pushing the boundaries of what is possible in the world of design, and to create products that truly make a difference in people's lives.
