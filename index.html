A bit about me...

Over the years in my career, I have had the opportunity to work in a variety of different industries. I have been able to apply my unique perspective to a range of challenges involving user experience and visual design, with a particular focus on building products in startup environments.
I aim to continually push the boundaries of what is possible in the world of design, and to create products that truly make a difference in people's lives and meet business expectations. I currently support design efforts at the PwC Experience Centre in Lagos, Nigeria.
